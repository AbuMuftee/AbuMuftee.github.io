Edurosy - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st June, 2023

We, Bezane Digitals, value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while using our educational mobile application, Edurosy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information, including email, name, profile picture, and data related to advertisements served through Google AdMob, for the purpose of authentication and personalization.

1. Information Collection and Usage

1.1 Authentication Data:

To provide a secure user experience and personalize your interactions within Edurosy, we collect and store the following information during the authentication process:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address to uniquely identify you and facilitate communication regarding your account.
  • Name: We collect your name to personalize your experience within Edurosy and display it within the app.
  • Profile Picture: We may offer you the option to upload a profile picture, which helps personalize your user experience.

1.2 Personalization:

We may use the information collected, including your email, name, and profile picture, to personalize your interactions within Edurosy. This includes but is not limited to customizing educational content, recommending relevant courses or learning materials, and providing tailored user experiences to enhance your educational journey.

1.3 Advertisements:

Edurosy serves advertisements through Google AdMob to support the app's free content and services. While serving these ads, AdMob may collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: AdMob may collect device-specific information, such as device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers, to deliver relevant ads and measure their performance.
  • Advertising Identifier: AdMob may collect and use the advertising identifier associated with your device to show personalized ads based on your interests.

2. Data Storage and Security

2.1 Storage:

We securely store your authentication data, including email, name, and profile picture, within our secure servers. This data is used solely for authentication purposes and to provide you with personalized features and experiences within Edurosy.

2.2 Google AdMob:

The information collected by Google AdMob for ad serving purposes is processed and stored on Google's servers. We do not have direct control over the data collected by AdMob. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your information.

2.3 Data Security:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data, and any transmission of information is at your own risk.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Firebase Authentication:

Edurosy utilizes Firebase Authentication, a third-party service provided by Google, to handle user authentication. The collection and processing of your authentication data by Firebase are subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google AdMob:

Edurosy serves advertisements through Google AdMob, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Google AdMob may collect and use data in accordance with their own privacy practices to serve personalized ads.

4. Children's Privacy

Edurosy is intended for users of all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it. If you believe we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us using the information provided in Section 6.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes made will be effective immediately upon posting the revised version in Edurosy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
